# Contacting Support

Need help? TheModernFamily team is here for you.

## How to Contact Support
- Use the **Help** or **Contact Support** option in the app.
- Or email us at [support@themodernfamily.app](mailto:support@themodernfamily.app)

## What to Include
- A description of your issue
- Steps to reproduce it (if possible)
- Screenshots or error messages

We'll get back to you as soon as possible!
