# Common Issues & Solutions

Here are solutions to some common problems in TheModernFamily.

## Login Issues
- Double-check your email and password.
- Use the password reset option if needed.

## Data Not Saving
- Make sure you have a stable internet connection.
- Try refreshing the page.

## Visualization Glitches
- Zoom in/out or refresh the canvas.
- If problems persist, contact support.

## Relationship Errors
- The app will warn you about circular or invalid relationships. Follow the prompts to fix them.
