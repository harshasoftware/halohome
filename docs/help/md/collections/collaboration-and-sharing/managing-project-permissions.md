# Managing Project Permissions

Control who can access and edit your family tree projects.

## Changing Roles
- Go to the **Collaborators** section in your project.
- Change a collaborator's role (viewer, editor, etc.) as needed.

## Removing Collaborators
- Click the **Remove** button next to a collaborator's name.

## Viewing Access History
- Some projects may show a log of who accessed or edited the tree.
