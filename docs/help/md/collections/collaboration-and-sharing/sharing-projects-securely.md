# Sharing Projects Securely

Share your family tree with others while keeping your data safe.

## Generating Share Links
- Click the **Share** button in your project.
- Choose the access level (view only, can edit, etc.).
- Copy the generated link and send it to your recipient.

## Setting Access Levels
- Decide if collaborators can view, comment, or edit your tree.

## Revoking Access
- You can revoke a share link or remove a collaborator at any time from the project settings.
