# Inviting Collaborators

You can invite others to help build your family tree in TheModernFamily.

## How to Invite
1. Open your project and go to the **Collaborators** section (usually in the sidebar or project menu).
2. Enter the email address of the person you want to invite.
3. Choose their role (viewer, editor, etc.) if available.
4. Click **Send Invitation**.

## Accepting Invitations
- Your collaborator will receive an email with a link to join your project.

## Tips
- You can manage collaborators and their permissions at any time.
