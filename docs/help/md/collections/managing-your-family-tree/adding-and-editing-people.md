# Adding and Editing People

People are the heart of your family tree. Here's how to manage them in TheModernFamily.

## Adding a New Person
1. Click the **Add Person** button (usually in the toolbar or by right-clicking the canvas).
2. Fill in details: name, birth/death dates, gender, avatar, etc.
3. Click **Save** to add them to your tree.

## Editing a Person
- Click on a person's node to open their profile dialog.
- Edit any details and click **Save**.

## Deleting a Person
- Right-click a person's node and select **Delete**.
- Confirm the deletion. (Note: This will also remove their relationships.)

## Merging People
- If you have duplicates, use the merge option in the person dialog or context menu.

## Tips
- You can add avatars for visual flair.
- Use the search bar to quickly find and edit people.
