# Creating and Managing Relationships

Relationships connect your family tree. TheModernFamily supports many types, including marriages, divorces, step-families, and more.

## Creating a Relationship
1. Drag from a handle on one person node to another, or use the **Add Relationship** button.
2. The **Relationship Type Modal** will appear.
3. Select the relationship type (parent-child, marriage, divorce, dating, etc.).
4. Fill in any extra details (dates, custom label).
5. Click **Save**.

## Editing or Removing a Relationship
- Click or right-click a relationship line to edit or delete it.
- You can change the type, dates, or remove the relationship entirely.

## Preventing Circular Relationships
- The app will warn you if you try to create a circular (impossible) relationship.
