# Exporting Your Family Tree

You can export or share your family tree from TheModernFamily in several ways.

## Exporting as GEDCOM
- Click the **Export** button in the toolbar or sidebar.
- Choose **GEDCOM** to download your tree as a .ged file for use in other genealogy apps.

## Exporting as Image
- Use the **Export as Image** option (if available) to save a snapshot of your tree.

## Sharing Your Tree
- See [Sharing Projects Securely](../collaboration-and-sharing/sharing-projects-securely.md) for how to generate share links and set permissions.
