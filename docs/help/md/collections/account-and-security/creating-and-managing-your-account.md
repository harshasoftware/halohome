# Creating and Managing Your Account

Manage your TheModernFamily account with ease.

## Creating an Account
- Click **Sign Up** on the homepage.
- Enter your email and create a password.
- Confirm your email if prompted.

## Logging In and Out
- Click **Log In** and enter your credentials.
- To log out, use the account menu in the app.

## Editing Your Profile
- Go to your profile/settings page to update your name, email, or avatar.
