# Data Encryption & Privacy

Your privacy is a top priority in TheModernFamily.

## How Your Data is Protected
- All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
- You can set passwords for extra protection on projects.

## Privacy Best Practices
- Only share your tree with trusted collaborators.
- Use strong, unique passwords for your account.

## Password-Protected Projects
- Enable password protection in project settings for sensitive trees.
