# Using the Globe Migration View

The globe view lets you visualize your family's migrations and locations over time.

## Accessing the Globe View
- Click the **Globe** icon in the view switcher.

## What You'll See
- Lines and markers show where family members were born, lived, or moved.
- Use the timeline scrubber to see migrations at different points in history.

## Filtering and Focusing
- Filter by person, date, or location using the sidebar.
- Click on a marker to see details about that person or event.

## Tips
- The globe view is great for exploring migration patterns and family history visually.
