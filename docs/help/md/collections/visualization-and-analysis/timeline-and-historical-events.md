# Timeline and Historical Events

The timeline view helps you see your family's story unfold over time.

## Navigating the Timeline
- Click the **Timeline** icon in the view switcher.
- Scroll or drag to move through time.

## Adding Events
- Click **Add Event** to attach a historical event to a person or family.
- Enter details (date, description, people involved).

## Filtering
- Use filters to show only certain types of events or people.

## Tips
- The timeline is useful for spotting patterns and understanding your family's history in context.
