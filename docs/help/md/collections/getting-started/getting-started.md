# Getting Started with TheModernFamily

Welcome to **TheModernFamily**! This guide will help you get started, introduce the app's main features, and show you how to begin building your family tree.

## What is TheModernFamily?
TheModernFamily is an interactive, privacy-focused app for building, visualizing, and sharing your family tree. It supports complex relationships, migration history, timelines, and secure collaboration.

## Key Features
- **Interactive Family Tree Canvas**: Add, edit, and visualize people and relationships.
- **Relationship Conventions**: Supports marriages, divorces, step-families, adoptions, and more.
- **Timeline & Globe View**: See your family's migration and history over time and space.
- **Collaboration**: Invite others to view or edit your tree.
- **Privacy & Security**: Your data is encrypted and protected.

## Supported Devices
- Modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Desktop, laptop, and most tablets

## Signing Up and Logging In
1. Go to the TheModernFamily homepage.
2. Click **Sign Up** to create a new account, or **Log In** if you already have one.
3. Follow the prompts to enter your email and password.

You're now ready to create your first family tree!
