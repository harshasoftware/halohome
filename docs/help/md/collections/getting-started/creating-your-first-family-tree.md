# Creating Your First Family Tree

Ready to start your family tree? Follow these steps to create your first project in TheModernFamily.

## 1. Create a New Project
- After logging in, click the **New Project** button (usually in the toolbar or main menu).
- Enter a name and (optionally) a description for your family tree.
- Click **Create**.

## 2. Explore the Workspace
- You'll be taken to your new tree's workspace.
- The main canvas is where you'll add people and relationships.
- Use the sidebar and toolbar for additional actions (import, export, settings, etc.).

## 3. Next Steps
- Add your first person (see [Adding and Editing People](./adding-and-editing-people.md)).
- Start building relationships (see [Creating and Managing Relationships](./creating-and-managing-relationships.md)).

You can create multiple projects and switch between them anytime from your dashboard.
